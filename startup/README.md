Bunyan is a PropTech-enabled platform designed to connect real estate project owners with investors through structured data, feasibility analysis, and intelligent matching.

The platform focuses on enabling better early-stage investment decisions by standardizing how real estate opportunities are presented, evaluated, and discovered.

---

## What Bunyan Is

Bunyan is a decision-support platform for real estate investment opportunities.

It helps:
- Project owners present their ideas in a structured and credible way  
- Investors discover opportunities aligned with their risk and return preferences  
- Reduce dependency on informal networks and non-standard feasibility reports  
- Enable early clarity before legal and financial engagement  

---

## Problem Statement

Real estate project owners often face:
- Difficulty accessing suitable investors  
- High cost and time required for feasibility studies  
- Lack of standardized evaluation criteria  
- Dependence on personal connections rather than project quality  

Investors face:
- Limited visibility into early-stage real estate projects  
- Inconsistent feasibility and ROI assessments  
- High due diligence overhead  
- Fragmented deal sourcing  

---

## Solution Overview

Bunyan provides a centralized platform where:
- Project owners submit structured project information  
- Feasibility and ROI indicators are generated using predefined logic  
- Projects are categorized by sector, risk level, and expected return  
- Investors discover or are matched with suitable opportunities  

---

## High-Level Workflow

1. Project owner submits project details  
2. Platform structures and validates the input data  
3. Feasibility and ROI indicators are generated  
4. Projects are categorized and scored  
5. Investors browse or receive matched projects  
6. Initial interest is expressed within the platform  
7. External legal processes take place  
8. Funding and escrow occur outside the platform  

---

## Core Value Proposition

- Data-driven feasibility instead of static reports  
- Transparent comparison between projects  
- Reduced friction in early-stage real estate funding  
- Structured discovery instead of manual sourcing

## Why Bunyan Is Different

Bunyan does not attempt to replace:
- Banks  
- Brokers  
- Legal entities  
- Escrow providers  

Instead, it focuses on:
- Improving decision quality before capital engagement  
- Enabling clarity, structure, and intelligent filtering
